Your Attorney

About Sonny Viswanath

Born of Indian descent, Sonny's parents immigrated to the United States before he was born. Growing up in the Metro Detroit area, he experienced firsthand the challenges Indian-American families face navigating legal, financial, and business matters in the U.S.

A graduate of Michigan State University (B.A., 2004) and Michigan State University Law School (J.D., 2007), Sonny has been practicing law since 2009. His diverse practice spans elder law, estate planning, litigation, business formation, and more.

Sonny is committed to not just representing his clients, but educating them — ensuring you understand every step of the legal process.
