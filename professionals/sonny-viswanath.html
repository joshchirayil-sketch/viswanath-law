S. K. Viswanath

Attorney & Founder — Viswanath Law Firm

Sandesh Kumar Viswanath, also known as Sonny, is from the Metro Detroit area. Born of Indian descent (Asian-Indian, not Native American), his parents immigrated to the United States before he was born.

Viswanath received his B.A. from the Eli Broad College of Business at Michigan State University in 2004 and his Juris Doctor from Michigan State University Law School (formerly known as Detroit College of Law) in 2007.

Viswanath started his law practice in 2009. His office is in Troy, MI. Other than practicing law, Viswanath enjoys traveling, reading non-fiction books, and spending time with his mixed-breed dog, Layla.
